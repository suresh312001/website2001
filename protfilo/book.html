About

"Love Is an Orphan" by Suresh Kumar is a poignant tale of love, resilience, and personal growth. Follow protagonist Harry Roy as he navigates life's challenges, exploring the depths of human emotion and the enduring power of love against the backdrop of a picturesque village. With rich characters and evocative storytelling, this book offers a heartfelt journey that lingers in the reader's heart long after the final page is turned.
